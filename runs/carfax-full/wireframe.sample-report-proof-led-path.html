Frequently asked

Are these really official Carfax reports?

Yes. Every report we deliver is the same official Carfax PDF you'd receive purchasing directly from carfax.com — same data sources, same format, same VIN coverage.

Why are you cheaper than carfax.com?

We purchase reports in bulk under a reseller arrangement and pass the volume savings to buyers. The product is identical; only the distribution path differs.

How fast is delivery?

Most reports arrive in 30-90 seconds. If your report hasn't arrived in 24 hours, you get a full refund — no questions.

What if my report is missing data?

Carfax data depends on what's been reported for that VIN. If the report we deliver is materially incomplete (no records), we'll refund.

What's your refund policy?

Full refund if (a) your PDF doesn't arrive in 24h, or (b) the report contains no records on the VIN. Submit via support email; processed within 1 business day.

How does dealer pricing work?

Volume tiers start at 50 reports/month. Each tier has a fixed per-report price. Net-30 billing available. API access for inventory tools at the 250+ tier.

Is my payment information safe?

Payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your card data. Optional PayPal checkout for buyers who prefer it.
