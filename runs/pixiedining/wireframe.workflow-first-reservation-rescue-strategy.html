Here's what happens after you save your alert

Pixiedining runs quietly in the background so you can stop refreshing the Disney app.

1

The booking window closes

Disney opens reservations 60 days ahead. The most-wanted tables book out in seconds. Cancellations trickle back into the pool unpredictably for weeks afterward.

2

You set your criteria

Restaurant, visit dates, and party size. (You already did this in the form above ↑.) We save your preferences — no Disney account needed.

3

We poll Disney continuously

Pixiedining checks Disney's availability feed every 30 seconds on Pro, every 90 seconds on Free. The moment a matching slot appears, we detect it.

4

Your alert fires instantly

You receive a push notification, SMS, or email — whichever you chose. The alert includes the restaurant, date, party size, and a direct link to Disney's reservation page.

5

You book on Disney's site

Click through to Disney's official reservation system and complete the booking yourself. We alert; we never log in or book on your behalf.

Your account. Your booking. Always.
