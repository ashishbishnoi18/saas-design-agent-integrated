FAQ

Common questions

Does Pixiedining log into my Disney account? ▼
No. Pixiedining never requests or stores your Disney account credentials. We monitor Disney's publicly available dining availability feed — the same data your browser sees when you search for reservations. We have no access to your account, booking history, or payment information.
Is this allowed? Is it against Disney's terms? ►
Pixiedining monitors the publicly available reservation feed and notifies users when a slot opens. We do not automate any booking actions, do not log into accounts, and do not interact with Disney's systems on users' behalf. Users complete all reservations directly through Disney's official site.
Do you guarantee I'll get a reservation? ►
No. Pixiedining alerts you the moment a slot matching your criteria appears in Disney's system. We cannot hold, secure, or book tables. The slot may also be visible to others — acting quickly after the alert is essential. On Pro, faster 30-second polling and auto-resume help maximize your chance.
What if I miss the alert? ►
Cancellation slots are competitive — most open slots on popular restaurants close within a few minutes. If you miss it, your alert stays active and will fire again when another slot appears. Pro users with auto-resume don't need to re-enable the alert each time it fires.
What is auto-resume? ►
Auto-resume is a Pro feature that automatically re-activates your alert after it fires. On the free plan, you need to manually re-enable the alert each time. On Pro, your monitoring continues without any action on your part — useful when multiple slots may open over several days.
What's the refund policy? ►
Pro subscriptions come with a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied within the first 7 days, contact us for a full refund. Monthly plans cancel at the end of the billing period. Annual plans are refundable in full within 7 days of purchase.
