Frequently asked questions

Is this safe — do I have to give you my Disney account?

No. Pixiedining never asks for your Disney username, password, or any account credentials. We monitor Disney's public availability feed — the same feed their app uses — and send you an alert when your table appears. You complete the booking yourself on Disney's official site.

How fast are the alerts?

Free alerts use standard polling — we check approximately every 5 minutes. Pro alerts use faster polling — every 60 seconds. On average, Pro users receive an alert within 4 minutes of a cancellation appearing. High-demand restaurants like Be Our Guest can re-book within 90 seconds, so faster polling meaningfully increases your chances.

What if a table never opens up?

Your alert runs until you cancel it or your trip date passes. We never charge you for an alert that didn't fire. Pro subscribers receive a full refund within 30 days if they're unsatisfied.

Does this work for Disneyland?

Yes. Pixiedining monitors 40+ Disneyland and Disney California Adventure dining locations including Blue Bayou, Carthay Circle, Napa Rose, and more. Select Disneyland from the restaurant search or the park filter.

How is Pixiedining different from MouseDining?

Pixiedining Pro polls every 60 seconds vs. MouseDining's 3–5 minute interval. We also include SMS alerts at no extra charge, cover both Walt Disney World and Disneyland in a single subscription, and offer auto-resume so monitoring restarts automatically if a table opens and closes before you can act.
