Common questions

Does Pixiedining ever log into my Disney account?
+
No — never. We monitor Disney's publicly available reservation feed. We never ask for your Disney login, password, or any account credentials. Your Disney account remains entirely separate from Pixiedining.
Will this guarantee I get the reservation?
+
No, and we want to be clear about that. We alert you the instant availability appears. You need to act quickly and book through Disney's official site. We cannot hold tables or book reservations on your behalf — that's not how Disney's system works.
How fast will I receive an alert?
+
Pro accounts check availability every 45 seconds; Free accounts every 3 minutes. SMS and push notifications typically arrive within seconds of us detecting an opening. Exact delivery time depends on your device and carrier.
What restaurants and parks are covered?
+
140+ restaurants across Walt Disney World and Disneyland Resort, including all high-demand table-service dining. See the full coverage list above.
What's your refund policy?
+
30-day money-back guarantee on Pro subscriptions, no questions asked. Cancel anytime from your account settings.
What do I do when I receive an alert?
+
Your alert includes a direct link to the booking page on Disney's official reservation site. Tap or click it immediately — availability can disappear within seconds or minutes. We recommend having Disney's My Disney Experience app ready on your phone.
