Is it safe? Common questions from first-time users

Do I need to give you my Disney account login?
No. Pixiedining never asks for your Disney username, password, or any Disney account credentials. We monitor publicly available dining availability data — no account access needed, ever.
What happens after I receive an alert?
You receive an email (or push notification, or SMS on Pro). Tap the link, log into your own Disney account, and complete the reservation yourself on Disney's official reservation site. Pixiedining does not book or hold tables on your behalf.
What if someone else grabs the table before I can?
Alerts go out the moment a slot appears. Multiple users monitoring the same restaurant may receive the same alert simultaneously — it's first-come, first-served on Disney's end. We can't hold or reserve anything. We can re-watch and alert you again if another slot opens.
Is there a refund if it doesn't help for my trip?
Pro comes with a 7-day full refund policy, no questions asked. The free tier has no payment — there's nothing to refund.
Is Pixiedining affiliated with Disney?
No. Pixiedining is an independent service and is not affiliated with, authorized, endorsed, or sponsored by The Walt Disney Company or its subsidiaries.
