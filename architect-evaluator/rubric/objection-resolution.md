# Objection Resolution Rubric

Score whether the architecture identifies and resolves the user's likely objections instead of merely presenting features.

Axes:

- `objections_identified`: Are the real blockers named in Section 4 and reflected in components?
- `objection_timing`: Do objections appear at the point where users would feel them?
- `trust_and_risk_resolution`: Are risk reducers, proof, guarantees, examples, or provenance placed with enough weight?
- `price_or_effort_clarity`: If price, setup effort, time, migration, or commitment are relevant, is the burden clarified before action?

Do not reward an FAQ dump at the bottom as objection resolution unless the timing and visibility actually match the decision sequence.
