# Business Outcome Fit Rubric

Score whether the architecture helps the intended audience complete the business-relevant decision or task.

Axes:

- `audience_fit`: Does the structure match the audience's knowledge level, motivation, and decision behavior?
- `business_goal_alignment`: Does the page structure move the stated business goal forward without adding generic filler?
- `conversion_or_task_path`: Is the path from comprehension to action or task completion visible and plausible?
- `proof_to_action_sequence`: Does proof appear before, near, or after action at the right moment for this strategy?

High scores require a concrete sequence that a user can follow. A visually polished page with generic proof and an isolated CTA should score as competent at best.
